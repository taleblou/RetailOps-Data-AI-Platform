# Stockout Intelligence Module

Stockout intelligence estimates near-term stockout exposure and highlights probable operational causes.

## Inputs

Inventory position, sales velocity, inbound supply, assortment context, and store or SKU hierarchy data.

## Outputs

Summary risk posture, SKU-level diagnostics, and artifacts that can feed replenishment planning.
