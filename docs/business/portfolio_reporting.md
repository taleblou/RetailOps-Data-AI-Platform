# Portfolio Reporting

Portfolio reporting combines outputs from multiple modules into an opportunity view across stores, regions, categories, or business units.

## Typical use cases

- compare where stockout risk, margin pressure, and retention risk overlap
- prioritize remediation budgets across the business
- give leadership one cross-module view rather than multiple specialist dashboards

Portfolio outputs should remain aggregation-focused and should point operators back to the underlying specialist modules when detailed action is required.
