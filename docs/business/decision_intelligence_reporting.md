# Decision Intelligence Reporting

Decision-intelligence reporting turns operational model outputs into ranked actions.

## Typical questions answered

- which replenishment actions should be prioritized this week
- which shipment delays require manual intervention now
- where promotion, retention, or returns actions have the highest expected business impact

## Inputs

This layer combines intelligence-module outputs, KPI context, and business rules. It is intended to support action queues, exception dashboards, and review-ready summaries.
