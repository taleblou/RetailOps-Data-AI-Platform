# Customer and Inventory Intelligence

This documentation group covers modules that explain customer behavior together with inventory posture.

## Included capabilities

- customer intelligence and customer cohorts
- customer churn and retention analysis
- inventory aging and ABC/XYZ classification
- stockout-adjacent assortment and substitution views

## Operating goal

Use these modules together when merchants need to connect demand quality, retention, assortment quality, and inventory exposure rather than studying each topic in isolation.
