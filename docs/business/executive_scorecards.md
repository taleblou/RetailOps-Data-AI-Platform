# Executive Scorecards

Executive scorecards summarize the platform for senior business and operations stakeholders.

## Expected sections

- revenue, margin, and order health
- demand and inventory risk posture
- service-level and fulfillment reliability
- model-governance and data-quality signals
- major actions, exceptions, and ownership notes

Scorecards should stay concise, trend-aware, and grounded in the same marts and artifacts that power operational dashboards.
