# Commercial Reporting

Commercial reporting packages revenue, margin, procurement, pricing, and retention signals for business stakeholders.

## Coverage

- promotion effectiveness and price-response summaries
- supplier procurement and purchase-order control views
- returns leakage and policy risk summaries
- cohort retention and customer-value trend reporting

## Delivery model

These reports are designed for periodic review cycles and dashboard publishing. They should be fed by trusted marts and module-produced artifacts so the same logic can support both APIs and stakeholder-facing outputs.
