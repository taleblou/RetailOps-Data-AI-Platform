# Business Review Reporting

Business review reporting is the umbrella reporting surface for recurring operating reviews.

## Scope

The module aggregates outputs from forecasting, replenishment, returns, supplier, promotion, customer, and governance workflows into review-ready artifacts.

## Typical outputs

- executive review decks or JSON payloads
- store, category, and SKU review summaries
- forecast-quality and inventory-position commentary
- cross-functional action lists for commercial and operations teams

## Dependency model

This reporting family should consume module artifacts and trusted marts rather than reaching into connector-specific raw tables.
